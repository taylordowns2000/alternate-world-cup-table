The Alternative World Cup 2026 Table

Points, goals, and goal difference for all 48 teams, per million people, per billions of dollars of GDP, or per GDP/capita.
· Explain

Why?

I've often found myself wondering how teams are performing based on a number of different factors. Population size (how many people might be able to play in a nation?), GDP (how large is that nation's economy by some inadequate but fairly standardized measure?), or GDP/capita (how wealthy are the people in that country?) and surprised that no basic, up-to-date table like this existed for WC2026.

This is a "live table". When a match is in progress it will show you the results as they would be if the match ended with no more goals. There's sometimes a slight delay because I'm using the free API from the good people at football-data.org ❤️
